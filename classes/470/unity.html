unity hw blog post

For one of our in class assignments, we messed around with using Unity as an active serial connection, and would send and return data from the UnityProject to the arduino.

This would result in the cube moving when the potentiometer is moved, and the red light turning on once the cube has moved past a certain threshold.

It was really fun to watch this come together and also watch a physical input have interactions within unity in realtime! Unity -> Arduino
